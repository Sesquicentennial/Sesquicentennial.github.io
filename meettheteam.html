Haley Hinze

Haley Hinze was born in Cascade, Idaho, a booming metropolis with a population exceeding 900 people. She worked mainly on the Android portion of this project, focusing on the History, Quests, and Memories features. When she isn’t holed up frantically pounding away at her worn-out keyboard she can be found skiing, ice skating, or backpacking in the wilderness because 900 people isn’t quite few enough. Haley has followed in the footsteps of her childhood hero Smokey the Bear and lives on top of a mountain looking for wildfires to report as part of her summer job as a fire lookout.
